About

Hello, fellow creatives!

I begin each project by listening to your business objectives and creative input. I align my skillset with the heartbeat of the story to activate viewer interest. Depending on your resources, I work both independently and as a part of a team. I am flexible.

My primary focus is documentary. I began my career in commercial post-houses, editing multi-screen auto-shows, TV, features, and non-profit. My full depth of experience allows me to bring an arsenal of style for transforming any project into a high-quality professional piece.

I am local to Los Angeles. My co-editors are my two chihuahuas: Ava and Lil' Peep. No job is too big or too small for us.

I spend my free-time volunteering for K-9 Youth Alliance, a local non-profit that supports kids in their efforts to train shelter dogs to increase their likelihood of finding a home. We are always looking for volunteers if you are interested. It's an enriching experience.

My other pastime is ongoing education. I have a BFA in film production, University of Colorado, Boulder. I am consistently learning new skills. I am currently enrolled in Code Institute's impressive Full-Stack web developer bootcamp.
